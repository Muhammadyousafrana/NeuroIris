About

This application trains a neural network entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js. All computation happens on your local CPU/GPU - no data is sent to any server.

Architecture: Dense neural network with 2 hidden layers (10 neurons each)

Optimizer: Adam with configurable learning rate

Loss Function: Categorical Crossentropy
